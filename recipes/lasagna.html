Lasagna

This is a simple, delicious lasagna, simple as that, it is not really hard to make, it is a simple pasta with meat, tomate sauce and cheese!

These are the ingredients required for this recipe:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

And, the steps to prepare the recipe are:

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the riccota mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
