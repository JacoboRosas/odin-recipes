Pizza

Pizza is always delicious, and even though it might be intimidating, you can prepare it in your home! (Or simply order if you're lazy, but then why are you here?)

These are the ingredients required for this recipe:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomate Sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese or any other cheese that you can use that is good for pizza
  • Toppings
  • Olive Oil

And, the steps to prepare the recipe are:

  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. Add the tomate sauce
  3. Add the cheese on top
  4. Add the toppings you like
  5. Bake
  6. Cut into slices
  7. Serve!
