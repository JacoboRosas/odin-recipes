Tacos

You can never go wrong with tacos, and there are multiple ways to make them!

These are the ingredients required for this recipe, however, you can really add anything, so I'll only describe the basic ingredients:

  • Tortillas
  • Meat
  • Toppings (Anything you want)
  • Sauce

And, the steps to prepare the recipe are:

  1. Prepare the meat, it can be anything you like
  2. Heat up the tortillas, so they are flexible and soft
  3. Prepare the sauce, better if it's anything hot
  4. Combine everything, first add the meat, then the toppings, and last, the sauce
